Brainstorming with Idea Boards helps you come up with new inspiration for your next project. Upload domain-specific thoughts and ideas, then combine them in new and interesting ways. We automatically save your brainstorming sessions, remembering all your ideas and making it easy to share your ideas.

Cards

Upload ideas in the forum of cards. Cards can contain anything from individual mechanics for a game to plot points.

Decks

Organize your Cards into Decks for brainstorming. Decks contain similar ideas so that the next time you need inspiration for a new song, you can check your “feelings” Deck.

Boards

Brainstorm ideas by picking some Decks and making connections between cards. Part presentation editor, part digital whiteboard, Boards let you freely experiment with ideas.
